China 中华人民共和国

The National Emblem of the People's Republic of China is a national symbol of the People's
Republic of China and contains in a red circle a representation of Tiananmen Gate, the entrance
gate to the Forbidden City, where Mao Zedong declared the foundation of the People's Republic
of China (PRC) in 1949. Above this representation are the five stars found on the national flag.
The largest star representsthe Chinese Communist Party (CCP),while the four smaller stars
represent the four social classes as defined in Maoism.The emblem is described as being "composed of patterns of the national flag"
